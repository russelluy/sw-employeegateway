• Global Navigation

    Navigation leading to all websites and resources on Safeway's intranet.

  • Primary Navigation

    Navigation for the Employee Gateway.

  • Stock Ticker

    A stock ticker of U.S. and Canada stock listings.

  • Featured Sites

    Links to a collection of featured Safeway resource websites.

    Career

    Takes you to the Safeway Careers website where you can search current job listings within the company.

    What's Cooking?

    A company-wide daily and weekly cafeteria menu.

  • Feature Focus

    Highlights a current company wide topic. The "Request+" allows you to submit a request for a feature under Feature Focus, Notices, Ads.

    Notices

    Directs you to a website where you can read the latest notices posted company-wide.

    Ads

    Allows you to view in-store promotions for both U.S. and Canada Safeway stores.

  • Navi-gator

    A section that provides you with links to training and learning.

    About Me

    Section that highlights the logged in user. Available are emoticons to choose from that describe the user's current mood. Can be seen by users who follow you.

  • Organizational Changes

    Information on the latest organization changes within the company. The "Request+" allows you to submit a request for an organizational change.

  • Featured News

    Latest news articles relating to Safeway. Clicking on "Request+" allows you to share relevent news.

    Industry News

    Latest news articles relating to the grocery industry.

    Rx

    Latest news articles relating to the pharmaceutical portion of the company.

  • Featured Videos

    Section that features important and current videos company-wide.

    Video Exchange

    Links you to the Safeway Video Exchange website where you can watch live video, upload videos, and see an archive of past videos.

    Broadcast Calendar

    A calendar that highlights dates of internal brodcasting events.

  • Question of the Week

    A weekly question users can answer regarding the company. Results can be viewed company wide. The "Request+" allows you to submit a possible future question of the week.

    Comments

    A popup box that allows you to make comments pertaining to the Employee Gateway.

  • People of the Week

    Featured profiles from different employees within the company and a chance to guess who they are and what they do.
